eatlistR

HackVictoria was a hackathon organized by Uvic WebDev. You had 24 hours (yes people pulled all-nighters in the ECS) to come up with something . EatlistR was an idea my friend and I thought would be perfect for broke university students like me! Basically, it's supposed to list all the amazing food deals in Victoria. Coded in Swift. Design and all graphics made by me. We won best design!
Excuse the low-res everything though. We were sleep deprived.
